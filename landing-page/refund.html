Refund Policy

Last updated: August 26, 2024

Important: NoWrong is currently a free platform. This policy covers potential future premium features and services.

1. General Policy

Generally, all sales are final. However, we understand that situations may arise where a refund is warranted.

Current Status: NoWrong is 100% free to use. No payments are required to access our core chat features.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered for:

  • Technical Issues: Platform malfunctions preventing service access
  • Billing Errors: Incorrect charges or duplicate transactions
  • Fraudulent Transactions: Unauthorized use of payment methods
  • Service Unavailability: Extended outages beyond our control

3. Subscription Refunds

(Applies to future premium features)

  • Refunds only available for unused subscription periods
  • No partial refunds for partially used subscriptions
  • Used subscriptions are not eligible for refunds
  • Automatic renewals can be cancelled to prevent future charges

4. In-App Purchase Refunds

(Applies to future virtual items)

  • Virtual items are generally non-refundable once used
  • Accidental purchases may be refunded if reported within 24 hours
  • Unused virtual items may be eligible for refund

5. Refund Request Process

How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Email us at info@contentladder.in

Step 2: Include the following information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Order or transaction ID
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund request
  • Supporting documentation (if applicable)

6. Processing Timeline

  • Review: 7-10 business days for decision
  • Processing: Approved refunds processed via original payment method
  • Timeline: 1-15 business days depending on payment provider
  • Notification: Email confirmation sent when refund is processed

7. App Store Purchases

For purchases made through app stores:

  • Google Play: Follow Google Play refund policies
  • Apple App Store: Follow Apple's refund procedures
  • Direct requests to the respective app store may be faster

8. Exceptions

No Refunds For:

  • Account terminations due to policy violations
  • Change of mind after service usage
  • Dissatisfaction with matches or conversations
  • Regional restrictions or access limitations

9. Consumer Protection

This policy complies with Indian consumer protection laws. If you're not satisfied with our refund decision, you may escalate through appropriate consumer forums.

10. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this refund policy. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app or email.
