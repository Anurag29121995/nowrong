Frequently Asked Questions

How does NoWrong protect my anonymity?
NoWrong never asks for or stores personal information like your real name, phone number, or address. All conversations are encrypted and your identity remains completely anonymous.
Is NoWrong really free to use?
Yes! NoWrong is 100% free. All core features including chatting, matching, and messaging are available without any charges.
How do I report inappropriate behavior?
You can report inappropriate behavior directly in the chat by tapping the report button, or email us at info@contentladder.in with details. We take all reports seriously.
Can I delete my account and data?
Yes, you can delete your account anytime from the settings menu. This will permanently remove all your data and conversations.
Is there an age requirement?
Yes, you must be 18 or older to use NoWrong. This is strictly enforced for the safety of all users.
How does matching work?
Our algorithm matches you with users based on shared interests, preferences, and desires. No lengthy profiles needed - just authentic connections.
